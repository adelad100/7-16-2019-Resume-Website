a bit about myself,

My name is Adeoluwa John Ijidakinro, and while I initially struggled to think about the first thing I want readers of my website to think about when they think of me, I settled on the word advocate. I am an advocate for underrepresented groups within the STEM field, whether it's by ethnicity, gender, or socioeconomic status and I firmly believe an essential part of any field is creating a pathway for that less fortunate to cross through. Being a first generation son of Nigerian immigrant parents, I realize day, by day the value of the pathways my parents carved for me to live within America, and over time its become my personal mantra. I aim to carry the torch my parents have passed to me and to create that same pathway to opportunity and education for all underprivileged individuals that follow my footsteps.
